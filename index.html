About Viva Kids World Preschool

At Viva Kids World Preschool, we provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where young minds embark on an exciting journey of learning. Our innovative, play-based curriculum fosters creativity, curiosity, and all-round development, ensuring that every child thrives.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every child is unique, and learning should be an enjoyable experience. Our curriculum blends Montessori principles with modern teaching techniques, focusing on emotional, cognitive, social, and physical development. Through a mix of structured learning and free play, we help children build confidence, independence, and a lifelong love for learning.

Why Choose Viva Kids World?

✔ Holistic Learning Approach - A balance of academics, creativity, and life

✔ Safe & Stimulating Environment - Child-friendly infrastructure designed to encourage exploration.

✔ Passionate & Experienced Educators-Well-trained teachers dedicated to early childhood education.

✔ Interactive & Engaging Activities - Storytelling, music, arts, crafts, hands-on learning, and outdoor play.

✔ Comprehensive Development - Focus on intellectual, emotional, and social well-being.

✔ Fun Events & Celebrations - Festivals, field trips, and special activity days to enhance learninge

Our Play School Programs

We offer personalized learning journeys for preschool kids to nurture creativity and curiosity.


At our pre-primary school, we have designed personalised learning journeys for preschool kids that ensure a holistic and engaging educational experience meant exclusively for nurturing their creativity and curiosity. Our baby play school has four programs, namely—Playgroup (Level 1), Nursery (Level 2), LKG (Level 3), and UKG (Level 4).

Playgroup
Our Playgroup program helps children explore and learn through fun activities.
Nursery
Exploring creativity through interactive learning.
LKG
Our LKG program prepares your child for formal schooling with a focus on language and cognitive skills.
UKG
Preparing for primary education with confidence.
